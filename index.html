A wise YouTuber once said: "The Legend of Zelda is an Action Adventure game series about exploring dungeons and being murdered by chickens". The series was created by Nintendo on the Nintendo Entertainment System, and has since been one of Nintendo's flagship franchises, along with Mario.

  • 1986: The Legend of Zelda (NES) and the very first game in the franchise is released.
  • 1987: The Legend of Zelda II: The Adventure of Link (NES) came out as a sequel to the original.
  • 1991: The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past (SNES)
  • 1993:
    • The Legend of Zelda: Link's Awakening (GameBoy Color) is released in June.
    • The Phillips CD-i games, including Link: The Faces of Evil and Zelda: The Wand of Gamelon, and Zelda's Adventure, were released on October.
  • 1998: The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time (Nintendo 64) is released.
  • 1999: Super Smash Bros. (Nintendo 64) features Link as a fighter in the game, and has remained in the series ever since.
  • 2000: The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask (Nintendo 64) is released as a direct sequel to Ocarina of Time.
  • 2001:
    • The Legend of Zelda: Oracle of Seasons and Oracle of Ages were released as twin games for the GameBoy Color on February.
    • Super Smash Bros. Melee (Nintendo Gamecube) was released on November/December, introducing Zelda, Sheik, Young Link, and Ganondorf as fighters and Link as a veteran.
  • 2002:
    • Four Swords, a game that was originally a minigame on the remastered version of A Link to the Past for the GameBoy Advance was released.
    • The Legend of Zelda: The Wind Waker (Nintendo GameCube) wasreleased.
  • 2003: Soulcalibur II featured Link as a guest fighter.
  • 2004:
    • The Legend of Zelda: The Minish Cap (GBA) was released.
    • The Legend of Zelda: Four Swords Adventure (Nintendo Gamecube/GameBoy Advance) is released, following the same formula as Four Swords.
  • 2006:
    • Freshly-Picked Tingle's Rosy Rupeeland, a spin-off of the Zelda series, was released in September.
    • The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess (Nintendo Wii) released in November.
  • 2007: The Legend of Zelda: Phantom Hourglass (Nintendo DS) was released as a direct sequel to The Wind Waker.
  • 2008: Super Smash Bros. Brawl (Wii) introduced Toon Link as a playable fighter, while every other Zelda fighter (except Young Link) returned.
  • 2009: The Legend of Zelda: Spirit Tracks (Nintendo DS) was released as a direct sequel to Phantom Hourglass.
  • 2011: The Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword (Nintendo Wii) was released.
  • 2013: The Legend of Zelda: A Link Between Worlds (Nintendo 3DS) as a sequel to A Link to the Past.
  • 2014:
    • Hyrule Warriors (Wii U), based on the game Dynasty Warriors, is released in August.
    • Mario Kart 8 (Wii U & Switch) features Link as a guest racer.
    • Super Smash Bros for Nintendo 3DS/Wii U (Nintendo 3DS/Wii U) is released late in the year, retaining the same cast selected from Super Smash Bros. Brawl.
  • 2015: The Legend of Zelda: Tri Force Heroes is released, following the same formula as the Four Swords games.
  • 2016: The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild (Nintendo Switch) was released.
  • 2018: Super Smash Bros. Ultimate was released, returning all Legend of Zelda fighters
  • 2019: Cadence of Hyrule (Switch) is released, featuring Adrisaurus and FamilyJules as guest composers.
  • 2020: Hyrule Warriors: Age of Calamity (Switch) is released, featuring elements from both Hyrule Warriors and Breath of the Wild.
